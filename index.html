    Projects

    Introducing A New Initiative- An Upcoming Mental Health Awareness Venture- SPECTRUM

    The word SPECTRUM refers to an umbrella for all sorts of psychological distress classified under clusters sharing similar clinical features. SPECTRUM is being launched as an initiative to spread mental health awareness in a broader perspective. The idea is to reach and break the stigma associate with mental health in each and every lay person. The collaborative effort behind this page comes from a team of young psychologists working in the field at a grassroot level. We aim to be a family to you. A safe space and provide you with a sense of comfort and dignity. Let's all come and find our own sweet spots under the shade of SPECTRUM and share our stories for a holistic understanding of the importance of psychological wellbeing.
